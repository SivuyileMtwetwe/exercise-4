About Urban Vault

Our Story

Urban Vault was born out of a passion for sourcing exceptional products that seamlessly blend form and function. As a lover of all things tech and fashion-forward, Sivuyile Mtwetwe curated an extensive collection of items that cater to the modern, metropolitan lifestyle.

"I wanted to create a space where people could discover exceptional jewelry, the latest gadgets, and on-trend clothing all under one roof," says Sivuyile. "Urban Vault is a reflection of my own aesthetic - a perfect balance of sophistication and edge."

Our Offerings

At Urban Vault, you'll find an ever-evolving selection of:

  • Jewelry: Unique, handcrafted pieces from local and global artisans
  • Electronics: The newest smartphones, tablets, wearables, and smart home devices
  • Apparel: Stylish, high-quality clothing and accessories for men and women

Our knowledgeable staff is always on hand to provide personalized recommendations and exceptional customer service. We take pride in curating products that are not only beautiful, but also functional and long-lasting.
